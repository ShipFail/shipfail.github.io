For Hackathon Organizers

Use Ship.Fail to turn idea chaos into velocity. No heavy platform, no lock‑in — just a lightweight hub your hackers will love.

  • Pre‑event idea seeding: participants publish and tag ideas weeks ahead, so teams self‑assemble.
  • Focus on building: keep Ship.Fail for ideas; continue using your favorite submission/judging tool.
  • Open badges: embeddable shields for repos: Built on Ship.Fail.
  • Zero cost, zero friction: as tiny and hacker‑friendly as js.org.

Example: Pre‑Event Flow

  1. Week −2: announce Ship.Fail link. Encourage posting/forking ideas.
  2. Week −1: enable Ready for Hackathon filter; teams form via Interested.
  3. Event day: kickoff; teams post final repo links; Ship.Fail badges added.

Optional: export CSV of public ideas for your schedule/Slack/Discord.
